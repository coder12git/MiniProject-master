Summary


Wood is a traditional packaging material with a moderate level of sustainability. It is valued for its natural appearance, durability, and biodegradability.


Wooden packaging products may include crates, pallets, and boxes. While wood is renewable and biodegradable, it requires careful sourcing to ensure sustainability.


Wooden packaging can have a significant carbon footprint and may involve energy-intensive processing. However, using sustainably sourced wood and optimizing manufacturing processes can reduce environmental impact.
